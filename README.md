# Palak-Patil1410-CARESYNC
## Description:
Chronic diseases such as diabetes, hypertension, and thyroid disorders require continuous monitoring, 
timely check-ups, and strict medication adherence. However, most patients struggle to manage their health 
information properly. Reports, prescriptions, and reminders often get scattered across paper files, different 
mobile apps, handwritten notes, and chats, making the entire process confusing and error-prone. This 
disorganization leads to missed medicines, delayed follow-ups, overlooked symptoms, and increased stress 
for patients and families. 


CareSync provides a unified digital platform to solve these challenges by bringing all essential aspects of 
chronic care into one place. It offers features such as medication reminders, health tracking, digital storage 
of medical records, and appointment alerts, along with easy communication between patients and doctors. 
By centralizing health data and automating key tasks, CareSync makes chronic disease management more 
organized, reliable, and convenient.

# **Objective**

 
The main objectives of CareSync are:• To automate daily health tasks such as medicine intake, sugar/BP 
tracking, and appointment reminders. 
 
• To create a centralized platform for storing reports, prescriptions, and medical histories. 
• To reduce manual errors and improve consistency in disease monitoring. 
• To offer clear and easy-to-read insights for both patients and doctors. 
• To ensure usability for all age groups, including elderly patients. 
• To enhance patient engagement by providing timely notifications and structured health information. 
• To support informed clinical decisions through accurate, well-organized, and continuously updated 
records. 
• To improve long-term disease management outcomes by promoting routine monitoring and adherence.

# **Work Flow**

1. **User Registration / Login**
      New users create an account.
      Existing users log in using credentials.
2. **Dashboard Access**
      After login, user enters the main dashboard.
      Dashboard shows all available features.
3. **Add / Manage Health Data**
      User can add patient details or personal health records.
      Data is stored and updated using the system.
4. **View Reports / Records**
      User can view stored health history and reports anytime.
5. **Update or Delete Data**
      Users can modify or remove existing records.
6. **Logout**
      User safely logs out of the system.
