Important Instructions for Diabetes Diet:

  • Morning Routine: Consume fenugreek seeds every morning on an empty stomach to help control blood sugar levels
  • Dairy Products: Avoid sugar, cream, and high-fat dairy products. Use low-fat milk or buttermilk without added sugar
  • Carbohydrates: Choose whole grains over refined carbohydrates. Include fiber-rich foods like oats, poha, and dalia
  • Protein Sources: Include pulses, grams, rajma, and sprouts in your meals for adequate protein intake
  • Vegetables & Fruits: Include plenty of vegetables and seasonal fruits in your diet. Have salads with every major meal
  • Portion Control: Follow the recommended quantities strictly. Do not exceed portion sizes
  • Meal Timing: Maintain consistent meal timings - breakfast (8-9 AM), lunch (12-1 PM), evening snack (4-5 PM), and dinner (8 PM)
  • Hydration: Stay hydrated with water and avoid sugary beverages. Herbal teas without sugar are recommended
  • Dinner: Have dinner by 8:00 PM with the same food items as lunch but in controlled portions
  • Cooking Methods: Avoid deep frying. Prefer boiling, steaming, or shallow frying with minimal oil
  • Regular Monitoring: Monitor blood sugar levels regularly and adjust diet as needed
  • Consultation: Always consult with your doctor or nutritionist for personalized adjustments based on your health condition
